ExportECON

Information:

Minerals:

Minerals include resources such as crude or refined oil, fuel, gas, mineral ores, electrical power, coal, etc. For instance, a country like Saudi Arabia would have minerals share a large part of its exports, because it exports a lot of petroleum.

Services:

Services usually include things like travel and tourism services, transport services (i.e. airlines, cruiselines), and information/communication tech.

Textiles:

Textiles of course include things like clothing, footwear, and also things like furniture (i.e. mattresses, chair seats), and paintings.

Agriculture:

Includes resources like meats, plant-based food, like wheat and barley, and tobacco.

Stone:

Includes resources like gold, ceramic items, and glassware.

Metals:

Metals include resources usually made out of either iron, steel, or aluminum.

Chemicals:

Chemicals include things like fertilizers, medicine, perfumes/colognes, and cleaning supplies.

Vehicles:

Vehicles include cars, ships, planes, and vehicle parts.

Machinery:

Machinery includes items like refrigerators, air condiitioners, fans, pumps, compressors, printers, and computers.

Electronics:

Electronics include items like apparatuses for communications, like radio or phone, telephones, heaters, generators, etc.

Other:

Commodities that weren't specified by a country.
